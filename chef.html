Meet Our Chefs

At Crustacean Creations, our chefs are the heart and soul of our pizzeria. With years of experience and a passion for culinary excellence, they craft each pizza with love and attention to detail. Our team is dedicated to using only the freshest ingredients to create mouthwatering flavors that keep our customers coming back for more.

Chef Mario

With over 15 years of experience in Italian cuisine, Chef Mario specializes in traditional Neapolitan pizzas. His secret dough recipe has been passed down through generations, ensuring the perfect base for every pizza.

Chef Lucia

Chef Lucia brings a modern twist to classic recipes. Known for her creative toppings and unique flavor combinations, she is always experimenting to bring new and exciting pizzas to our menu.

Chef Giovanni

As a master of wood-fired cooking, Chef Giovanni ensures that each pizza is cooked to perfection in our traditional wood-fired oven, giving it that authentic taste and texture.

The Art of Pizza Making

Creating the perfect pizza is an art that requires skill, patience and passion. We love what we do and pass this love on to the whole world. We try to ensure that the inherited traditions live forever and delight you.

Our Team: A Family of Pizza Lovers

At our pizzeria, we pride ourselves on having a warm and friendly team that feels more like a family. Each member brings their unique skills and passion for pizza, ensuring that every pie we create is a masterpiece. Our team works collaboratively, sharing tips and techniques to constantly improve our craft.

Student Internships

We believe in nurturing the next generation of pizza makers. Our internship program allows students to gain hands-on experience in a real-world kitchen environment. Under the guidance of our seasoned chefs, interns learn the intricacies of pizza making, from dough preparation to the art of topping selection.

Natural Ingredients

We are committed to using only the finest natural ingredients. Our dough is made fresh daily, and we source our vegetables and meats from local farms. This not only enhances the flavor of our pizzas but also supports our community and promotes sustainable practices.
